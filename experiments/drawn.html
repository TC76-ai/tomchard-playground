What goes into one.

i

Type that carries the voice.

One serif, chosen deliberately, doing everything from whisper to headline. The typography on this very page is the same family your site would use — set with intent, not defaults.

ii

Motion with a purpose.

Reveals, parallax, drawn lines — but only where they help someone understand the page. Everything respects visitors who ask for reduced motion. Restraint is part of the craft.

iii

Fast on a phone in a field.

Hand-written code with no framework weight. Your customers are on mobile data outside the property they want you to work on — the site loads before they lose patience.

iv

Yours, outright.

No subscriptions holding the site hostage. When it's done it's handed over — files, domain, the lot — with a walkthrough of how everything works.

A template can be opened. A website can only be made — by someone who asked about your business first.
